Why Greener ?

            According to Global Climate Change Organization (G.C.C.O), over 315,000 people die annually due to drastic climate changes globally. The drastic climate changes are majorly attributed to the activities of man e.g. deforestation, dumping of industrial wates, emission of toxic fumes into the atmosphere, among others. The negative climate changes have not only affected humans but animals too i.e, some animal species have almost gone extinct, many have lost their habitats and many others are prone to diseases which wasn't the case until now. And that is why we need you to join the campaign to help us combat the negativity of climate change through this platform "Greener". So, register now in order to share you on how some of these plagues can be solved to save our future. Remember to keep our planet 'Greener'.
